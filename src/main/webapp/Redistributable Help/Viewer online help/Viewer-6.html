Step 4 – Accessing additional information

Data sources can store huge amounts of data – not all of it necessarily about relationships. Companies may have annual reports or contact details available online, or the data source may store the source document behind a particular relationship on the chart, such as the full details of a financial transaction.

You may be able to access this information from within the iXv Viewer. If the data provider has implemented this functionality, you can right-click an entity to open the shortcut menu and select an appropriate menu command (for example, More Information or View Meeting Report).

The additional information is displayed in your web browser.
